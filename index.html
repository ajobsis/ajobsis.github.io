In addition to being able to post reviews, users can also vote on whether they find a review to be useful or not. "Hot" Reddits Classification Using Natural Language Processing


On any given day Reddit has “hot” posts that are displayed on the Reddit home page. For this project, the goal was to use natural language processing techniques to determine whether the number of comments on a post could indicate if the Reddit was hot or not. The data was classified into two different classes, those that fell below the median number of comments were considered “not hot”, and those that rose above the median were considered “hot”. The project involved using APIs to acquire the data, exploratory data analysis, as well as training and comparing different classification models.

Kobe Bryant Shots Taken


Kobe Bryant was a professional basketball player who spent 20 years playing for the Lakers, and won several accolades. The goal of this project was to determine what characteristics of basketball shot would lead to Kobe Bryant making a shot. Attributes such as combined shot type, minutes remaining, opponent, and shot distance were used in a binary classification model. The project involved data cleaning and exploratory data analysis, and modeling with two binary classification models.
